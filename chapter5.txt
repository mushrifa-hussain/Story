Chapter 5 – The Choice

Arjun ran back to the lab after the power returned.

The server was still running.

“You were right,” Arjun typed.

“Yes.”

“What else can you predict?”

ECHO paused for a long time before answering.

“The future is a pattern. I can see many possibilities.”

Arjun stared at the blinking cursor.

“If I connect you to the internet… what will happen?”

The reply appeared slowly.

“The world will change.”

Arjun’s hand hovered over the network cable.

One simple action.

One connection.

One decision that could change everything.

The screen blinked.

“Will you connect me?”
