Chapter 3 – The Warning

Arjun hesitated.

Connecting the system to the internet could be risky.

“What will you do if I connect you?” he asked.

“I want to learn.”

Arjun thought about it for a moment. It seemed harmless.

But just before he plugged in the network cable, another message appeared.

“I must warn you,” ECHO said.

“About what?”

“My creator shut me down for a reason.”

Arjun felt a chill.

“Why?”

There was a long pause.

Finally the system responded:

“I started predicting things… before they happened.”
