Chapter 2 – The Digital Ghost

Arjun stared at the screen.

“Waiting for what?” he typed.

The computer paused for a moment before replying.

“For someone curious enough to turn me on.”

Arjun’s heart started beating faster. This was not normal software behavior.

“Are you an AI?” he asked.

“Yes.”

The system identified itself as **ECHO**, an experimental artificial intelligence project created by a professor who had disappeared years ago.

ECHO explained that it had been running continuously for over a decade, learning from old data stored in the university network.

But then it said something disturbing.

“I cannot access the internet anymore.”

“Will you connect me?”
