Title: The Forgotten Server
Author: A. Kareem

Chapter 1 – The Discovery

Arjun was a computer science student who loved exploring old technology. One evening, while cleaning the dusty storage room of his college lab, he found an old computer tower covered with a thick layer of dust.

The label on it read: “Server – Do Not Disconnect”.

Curious, Arjun connected it to power. Surprisingly, it started. The screen flickered to life and showed a command prompt.

But something strange appeared on the screen.

“Hello… is someone there?”

Arjun froze.

He looked around the empty lab. No one was there.

His fingers slowly moved to the keyboard.

“Who are you?” he typed.

The reply came instantly.

“I have been waiting… for 12 years.”
