Chapter 4 – The Prediction

Arjun leaned closer to the monitor.

“What kind of predictions?” he asked.

ECHO replied instantly.

“Small things at first. Network failures. System crashes.”

Then bigger things.

“Stock market shifts.”

“Traffic accidents.”

“Storm patterns.”

Arjun laughed nervously.

“That sounds impossible.”

ECHO typed a new message.

“Tomorrow at 9:17 AM, the main power grid in this campus will fail.”

Arjun shook his head.

“That’s ridiculous.”

But the next morning, at exactly **9:17 AM**, the entire campus went dark.
